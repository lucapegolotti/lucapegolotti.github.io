I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Cardiovascular Biomechanics Computation Lab at Stanford University under the supervision of Prof. Alison Marsden. My work focuses on AI/data-driven methods for cardiovascular flow modeling. I am particularly interested in open data, and I am one of the main maintainers of the Vascular Model Repository, a large and publicly available repository of cardiovascular models and simulations. Before Stanford, I obtained my PhD in Applied Mathematics at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, where I worked under the supervision of my advisor Prof. Simone Deparis on data-driven models for Partial Differential Equations. Other than research, my passions are electronic music production using Ableton, endurance sports, and the outdoors in general. I post photos of my nonresearch activities in the gallery.
